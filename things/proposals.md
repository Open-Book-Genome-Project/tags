# things — Proposals

`things` is an open/growing type. Tags are created per-work as needed. This file tracks scope decisions about what belongs in `things` vs. adjacent types.

See [AGENTS.md](../AGENTS.md) for the definition of `things`.

---

## Scope Decisions

### Creatures vs. Animals
- **Decision:** 2026-04
- **Rule:** Real animals that characters physically interact with → `things` (dogs, horses, ravens). Fantastical creatures that play a role analogous to animals → `things` (dragons, unicorns, familiars). Abstract animal symbolism or ecological topics → `sub_topics` or `literary_themes`.

### Weapons: specific vs. generic
- **Decision:** 2026-04
- **Rule:** Be as specific as is meaningful and common. "Pistols" is better than "guns". "Longsword" is better than "weapons". But "a sharp object" is too vague. Use the term a knowledgeable reader would search for.

### Technology in Sci-Fi
- **Decision:** 2026-04
- **Rule:** Fictional technology that serves as a prop (the spaceship characters travel in, the laser the villain fires) → `things`. Technology as a *subject* (what AI means for humanity) → `main_topics`. Specific named technologies central to the plot (The One Ring, the Holodeck) → `things` with the specific name.
