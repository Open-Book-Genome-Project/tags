# Audience — Controlled Vocabulary

A managed list. To propose additions or changes, see [CONTRIBUTING.md](../CONTRIBUTING.md).

Tag format: `audience:term` (e.g., `audience:young-adult`)

---

| Tag | Approx. Age | Definition |
|---|---|---|
| Preschool | 0–4 | Picture books, board books, read-aloud material |
| Children | 5–8 | Early readers, illustrated stories, early chapter books |
| Middle Grade | 8–12 | Chapter books with more complex plots and themes |
| Young Adult | 13–18 | May include mature themes; stops short of adult content |
| Juvenile | Under 18 | Catch-all when more specific age tier is unavailable; maps to legacy OL "Juvenile fiction" |
| Adult | 18+ | General adult readership |
| Academic | — | Scholarly/research contexts; assumes subject background |
| Professional / Technical | — | For practitioners; assumes professional expertise |
| General / All Ages | — | Intentionally accessible across age groups |
