# Content Warnings — Controlled Vocabulary

Strictly controlled. New additions require maintainer ratification via GitHub Issue before a PR will be accepted.

Tag format: `content_warning:term` (e.g., `content_warning:graphic-violence`)

---

| Tag | Definition |
|---|---|
| Abuse (emotional) | Sustained depiction of psychological or emotional abuse between characters |
| Abuse (physical) | Sustained depiction of physical abuse, battery, or bodily harm between characters |
| Abuse (sexual) | Depiction of sexual abuse or molestation |
| Addiction / Substance use | Depiction of addiction, substance dependency, or significant drug/alcohol misuse |
| Animal harm | Depiction of cruelty to, torture of, or graphic death of animals |
| Child death | Death of a child depicted on or near the page |
| Domestic violence | Depiction of violence within intimate or family relationships |
| Eating disorders | Depiction of disordered eating, anorexia, bulimia, or related conditions |
| Graphic violence | Detailed, explicit depictions of physical violence, gore, or bodily harm |
| Homophobia / Transphobia | Significant depiction of anti-LGBTQ+ discrimination, slurs, or violence |
| Racism / Racial slurs | Significant depiction of racism, racial violence, or use of racial slurs |
| Self-harm | Depiction of self-inflicted injury or self-destructive behavior |
| Sexual assault | Depiction of rape or sexual assault |
| Sexual content (explicit) | Explicit, graphic sexual content |
| Slavery | Depiction of chattel slavery and its conditions |
| Suicide | Depiction of suicide or suicide attempts |
| Torture | Depiction of deliberate infliction of severe pain or suffering |
| War violence | Graphic depiction of combat, casualties, or atrocities of war |
